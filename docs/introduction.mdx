## Why Plugwright?

MockBukkit doesn't support NMS and GUI. We created Plugwright because testing plugins that rely on real mechanics, GUIs, and NMS was painful. With Plugwright, you spin up a real server, inject real player bots, and test your plugins end-to-end.
